## âœ… TESTING YOUR ADAPTED PROMPT

### Good Signs (Your prompt will work well):
- Contains "background" or "underscore"
- Contains "minimal" or "subtle"
- BPM range is 10-15 BPM wide
- Has 2-3 specific genres
- Has 2-3 specific moods
- Under 200 characters (or close)

### Warning Signs (May get too many results):
- No "background" keyword
- BPM range > 20 BPM
- Generic genres ("electronic" instead of "minimal techno")
- No mood keywords

### Bad Signs (Will get wrong results):
- Technical terms (RMS, dB, Hz, dynamic range, waveform)
- Duration mentioned (3 minutes, 180 seconds)
- Too many requirements (Epidemic gets confused)
