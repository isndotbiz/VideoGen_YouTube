### STEP 2: Search for Music (2 minutes)

1. Click in the search box at the top
2. Copy and paste this:
   ```
   electronic instrumental background music for tech tutorials chillstep minimal techno 110-130 BPM energetic modern no vocals
   ```

5. Click **"Instrumental"** filter on the left sidebar

### STEP 3: Pick a Track (1 minute)

Look at the track names. Pick one that sounds minimal/ambient:

**Good Names (suggest consistency):**
- Anything with: Ambient, Minimal, Calm, Flow, Drift, Float, Soft
- Examples: "Diffuser," "Wander," "Daydreamer"

**Avoid Names (suggest drama):**
- Anything with: Drop, Explosion, Rise, Build, Peak, Chaos

**Action:**
1. **Write down the track name** (example: "Diffuser")
2. **Move to Step 4**

---

### STEP 4: Go to Labs Adapt (1 minute)

1. In the address bar, type: **https://www.epidemicsound.com/labs/adapt/**
2. Press **Enter**
3. Wait 4 seconds for page to load
4. You should see "Adapt length" and "Adapt music" buttons

**If you see a track already loaded:**
- Click **"New"** button (top area)
- Wait 2 seconds

---

### STEP 5: Search for Your Track (1 minute)

1. Find the **search box** (should say "Search" or have a magnifying glass)
2. Click in it
3. Type the track name you wrote down (example: **Diffuser**)
4. Press **Enter**
5. Wait 4 seconds
6. Click the **first result** (should match your track name)
7. Wait 4 seconds for track to load
8. You should see a waveform appear

---

### STEP 6: Adapt Length (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Adapt length"** button
2. Wait 2 seconds for panel to open
3. Find the duration box (shows a number)
4. Click in it and type: **180** (this is 3 minutes in seconds)
5. Look for a checkbox that says **"Ducking mix"** or **"Ducking"**
6. **Check the box** if it's not already checked (ducking auto-lowers music during voice)
7. Click the **"Adapt"** button (usually blue/primary color)
8. **WAIT 1-2 MINUTES** - AI is processing
9. When done, you'll see either:
   - "Download" button appears, OR
   - "Adapt music" button appears

---

### STEP 7: Adapt Music (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Adapt music"** button
2. Wait 2 seconds for panel to open
3. Find the text box (might be labeled "Description" or show placeholder text)
4. Click in it and paste this:
   ```
   Minimal background-friendly for voiceover. Reduce mid-range frequencies. Subtle and consistent.
   ```
5. Look for a dropdown that says **"Stems"** or **"Select stems"**
6. If you see it, select **"All stems"**
7. Click the **"Adapt"** button
8. **WAIT 1-2 MINUTES** - AI is processing again
9. When done, **"Download"** button appears

---

### STEP 8: Download WAV (1 minute)

1. Click **"Download"** button
2. Wait 1 second - a dialog pops up
3. Look for format options: **MP3** and **WAV**
4. Click **"WAV"** (higher quality)
5. Click the final **"Download"** button
6. Wait 10-30 seconds for file to download
7. File saves to your Downloads folder
8. **File size should be 30-80 MB** (if smaller, something went wrong)

---

### STEP 9: Organize the File (1 minute)

1. Go to your Downloads folder
2. Find the WAV file (might be named like "Diffuser_adapted.wav")
3. Rename it to something clear: **"Diffuser_3min_tech_background.wav"**
4. Move it to: **D:\workspace\VideoGen_YouTube\background_music_epidemic\**
