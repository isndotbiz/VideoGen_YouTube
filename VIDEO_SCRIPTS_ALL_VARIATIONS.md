## AUDIO PRODUCTION NOTES

### For All Scripts:

**Recording Settings:**
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz minimum
- Bit depth: 16-bit minimum
- Microphone: USB condenser recommended
- Room: Quiet, no echo

**Post-Production:**
- Normalize audio to -3dB
- Remove background noise
- Add subtle compression (ratio 4:1, threshold -20dB)
- Equalize: Slight boost at 2-3 kHz for clarity

**Music:**
- Background music: -25dB (20dB below voice)
- Volume automation if voice dynamics are high
- Fade in/out at beginning and end

**Pacing:**
- Natural pauses between sections (1-2 seconds)
- Emphasis on key points (slightly louder, slower)
- Variation in tone to maintain interest
