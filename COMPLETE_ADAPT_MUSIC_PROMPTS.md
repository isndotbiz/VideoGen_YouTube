### FOR TECH TUTORIALS

#### Version 1: Ultra-Minimal (Maximum Voice Clarity)

```
Minimal ambient background texture with soft pad layers and gentle harmonic
drones. Very sparse arrangement with extremely low mid-range frequencies
(200-800Hz completely rolled off). No percussion, no melodic elements, no
rhythm section. Ultra-subtle atmospheric layers that sit far in the background.
Consistent gentle energy throughout with no buildups, no drops, no dynamic
changes. Soft high-frequency shimmer and deep sub-bass foundation only. Perfect
for continuous background presence that never competes with voiceover. Calm,
peaceful, barely noticeable.
```

**Best For:**
- Information-dense tutorials
- 90%+ talking
- Complex topics requiring full attention
- Professional educational content

**Expected Result:** Music at ~10% volume, nearly imperceptible

---

#### Version 2: Moderate (â­ RECOMMENDED FOR MOST VIDEOS)

```
Smooth ambient electronic background with soft synth pads and subtle rhythmic
elements. Gentle filtered percussion with mid-range frequencies (200-800Hz)
significantly reduced for voice clarity. Light melodic movement in high
frequencies only. Consistent moderate energy throughout with no dramatic
buildups or drops. Minimal bass presence, focus on atmospheric textures. Smooth
transitions, even dynamics, background-friendly arrangement. Professional and
clean sound that supports voiceover without competing. Tech-focused, modern,
approachable.
```

**Best For:**
- Standard tech tutorials
- 70-90% talking
- Engaging but educational content
- YouTube tutorials, online courses

**Expected Result:** Music at ~15% volume, present but subtle

#### Version 3: Energetic (Noticeable Energy)

```
Upbeat electronic background music with steady rhythm and filtered mid-range.
Light percussion groove with muted frequencies between 200-800Hz to preserve
voice clarity. Bright melodic elements in high frequencies, subtle bass
foundation. Consistent energetic feel throughout with no sudden buildups or
drops. Smooth even dynamics, background-optimized mixing. Modern tech sound with
motivational energy that stays supportive of voiceover. Clean production with
space for dialogue, professional broadcast-ready balance.
```

**Best For:**
- Product demos
- Exciting tech announcements
- 50-70% talking
- Fast-paced tutorials

**Expected Result:** Music at ~20% volume, energetic but controlled

### FOR BUSINESS/CORPORATE VIDEOS

#### Version 1: Ultra-Minimal Corporate (Presentations)

```
Ultra-minimal background track for professional corporate presentation. Maximum
```

## ðŸ“– EPIDEMIC'S OFFICIAL BEST PRACTICES {#best-practices}

### From https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/how-to-use-adapt/

#### 1. Describe the Sound You Want (Not the Changes)
**Good:** "Tense, dark, and sophisticated jazz noir"
**Bad:** "Make it darker and more tense"

#### 2. Use Epidemic's Tags as Foundation
Start with their genre/mood/instrument tags, then expand:
- Genre: chillstep, minimal techno, corporate
- Mood: calm, energetic, professional
- Instruments: piano, synth pads, strings

#### 3. Use Emotive, Descriptive Language
**Good:** "Heavy & ponderous electric guitar with epic, cinematic mood"
**Bad:** "Guitar music"

#### 4. Specify Intended Use
Help AI understand context:
- "For podcast background"
- "For documentary narration"
- "For corporate presentation"

#### 5. Experiment and Iterate
First attempt may not be perfect - try variations

## ðŸ”„ COMPLETE WORKFLOW {#workflow}

### STEP-BY-STEP (All in One Place)

**STEP 1: Search for Track (2 minutes)**

1. Go to: https://www.epidemicsound.com/music/search/
2. Paste SEARCH PROMPT from your video type (see section above)
3. Press Enter
4. Click "Instrumental" filter
5. Pick a track (15-25 results)
6. Write down track name

---

**STEP 2: Navigate to Labs Adapt (1 minute)**

1. Go to: https://www.epidemicsound.com/labs/adapt/
2. Wait 3 seconds
3. If track loaded: Click "New" button
4. Wait 2 seconds

---

**STEP 3: Search for Track (1 minute)**

1. Find search box
2. Type track name
3. Press Enter
4. Click first result
5. Wait 4 seconds for track to load

---

**STEP 4: Adapt Length (2 minutes)**

1. Click "Adapt length" button
2. Enter duration: **180** (3 minutes in seconds)
3. Find "Ducking mix" checkbox
4. **CHECK the ducking box** â† IMPORTANT for voiceover!
5. Click "Adapt" button
6. WAIT 1-2 minutes for AI processing

---

**STEP 5: Adapt Music (2 minutes)**

1. Click "Adapt music" button
2. Paste ADAPT MUSIC PROMPT from your video type (see section above)
3. Select "All stems" (if available)
4. Click "Adapt" button
5. WAIT 1-2 minutes for AI processing

---

**STEP 6: Download (1 minute)**

1. Click "Download" button
2. Select "WAV" format
3. Click final "Download" button
4. Wait for file (30-80 MB)
5. Done!

**Total Time:** ~10 minutes per track

## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### Tip 1: Always Mention "Voiceover" or "Narration"
