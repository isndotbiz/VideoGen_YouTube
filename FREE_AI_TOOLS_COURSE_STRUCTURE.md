## BENEFITS OF THIS COURSE STRUCTURE

1. **Shorter = Higher Completion Rate**
   - 3:30 videos = 90% watch rate
   - 1 long video = 40% watch rate
   - More people = more revenue

2. **SEO Optimized**
   - 4 videos = 4 times the YouTube search visibility
   - Each video ranks for different keywords
   - More traffic overall

3. **Promotion Easier**
   - Each video = new promotion opportunity
   - Creates anticipation/series effect
   - "Next video tomorrow" = engagement boost

4. **Course Feel**
   - Students feel like they're learning progression
   - "Video 1 of 4" = commitment signal
   - Higher perceived value

5. **Multi-Platform Friendly**
   - 3:30 videos work great on TikTok (can post full)
   - Works on Instagram Reels
   - Works on YouTube
   - Easy to clip short 15-30 sec segments for shorts
