## üéß **What to Listen For (Electronic Edition):**

### ‚úÖ **GOOD Electronic Tracks:**
- Steady, driving rhythm (not chaotic)
- Bass is present but not overpowering
- Melody is clear but not distracting
- No sudden volume spikes or drops
- Clean, modern production
- You can still hear yourself talking over it
- Consistent energy throughout (no build-up ‚Üí drop ‚Üí quiet ‚Üí repeat)

### ‚ùå **BAD Electronic Tracks:**
- Heavy bass drops every 16-32 bars
- Volume fluctuates dramatically
- Bass frequencies compete with voice frequencies
- Too many layers (sounds crowded)
- Aggressive, harsh sounds
- Makes you want to headbang (too distracting!)

## üéöÔ∏è **Special Considerations for Electronic Music:**

### **Bass Frequency Conflict:**

Electronic music has lots of low-end bass. Your voice is mid-range.

**Solution 1:** Download with **Ducking Mix** (auto-reduces bass during voice)

**Solution 2:** When mixing, apply **High-Pass Filter** to music:
```python
# Remove frequencies below 100 Hz (where voice doesn't live)
from pydub import AudioSegment
music = AudioSegment.from_file("electronic_track.wav")
# This helps voice cut through
```

**Solution 3:** Choose tracks with **less sub-bass** (150-200 Hz instead of 40-80 Hz)

## üí° **Pro Tips for Electronic Music:**

### **Avoiding Bass Overload:**

1. **Preview with headphones** (bass is more obvious)
2. **Look for tracks labeled:**
   - "Minimal"
   - "Melodic"
   - "Atmospheric"
   - "Liquid"
   - "Chill"
3. **Avoid tracks labeled:**
   - "Heavy"
   - "Aggressive"
   - "Hard"
   - "Banger"

### **Finding Consistent Sections:**

Electronic tracks often have structure:
```
[Intro] ‚Üí [Build] ‚Üí [Drop] ‚Üí [Break] ‚Üí [Build] ‚Üí [Drop] ‚Üí [Outro]
  30s       15s      30s       20s       15s      30s      20s
```

**Best section to download:**
- **AFTER first drop** (around 1:00-1:15)
- **Duration:** 90 seconds from there
- **Avoids:** Quiet intro, dramatic builds, outro fade

**In Adapt tool, set:**
```
Start: 1:00 (60 seconds)
Duration: 90 seconds
Result: 1:00-2:30 (the "body" of the track)
```
